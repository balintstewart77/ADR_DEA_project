## 1. Stage 1 — expression-of-interest email

Subject: Would you be interested in helping validate the DEA register portfolio view?

Dear {{RESEARCHER_NAME}},

We are developing a portfolio view of projects in the public DEA register. The
public dashboard is available at {{DASHBOARD_URL}}. The view groups register
projects using classifications produced with a large language model. The formal
validation examines whether those proposed classifications fit the actual
projects and whether their basis is visible in the public register.

Researchers named on several DEA projects are particularly valuable because
their project knowledge provides evidence unavailable from blind register-only
coding. You are associated with {{PROJECTS_OFFERED}} eligible project(s). Each
review is expected to take about {{MINUTES_PER_PROJECT}} minutes, or about
{{ESTIMATED_TOTAL_MINUTES}} minutes if you reviewed all of them. You may review
all, some or none.

Would you be interested in receiving the participant information and one-time
consent form? This message is only an expression-of-interest enquiry. It is not
informed consent and contains no project-review link.

Contributors may separately choose to be acknowledged by name in resulting
outputs; acknowledgement never depends on reviewing every project, agreeing
with a classification, or permitting quotations.

Kind regards,

{{STUDY_NAME}} team
