## 1. Initial expression-of-interest email

Subject: Would you be interested in helping validate the DEA register portfolio view?

Dear {{RESEARCHER_NAME}},

We are developing a portfolio view of projects in the public DEA register. The
register dashboard is available here: {{DASHBOARD_URL}}. The portfolio view is
intended to make the public register easier to understand by grouping projects
by research domain, analytical purpose and two cross-cutting tags.

The proposed classifications were produced using a large language model. The
formal validation will assess how well those classifications represent the
projects, what can reasonably be inferred from the public register entry, and
where the register entry or taxonomy limits interpretation. Blind register
coding cannot provide the project knowledge held by researchers who worked on
the projects, so project-researcher evidence is a valuable complementary input.
It will be treated as project-informed validation evidence, not as a definitive
gold standard.

Researchers associated with several DEA projects are particularly valuable
because one person's optional input can cover multiple projects and help assess
whether the portfolio classifications work consistently across different
entries, while still allowing that person to review only the projects they
choose.

You are associated with {{ELIGIBLE_PROJECT_COUNT}} eligible projects and would
be offered {{PROJECTS_OFFERED}} short project reviews. Each review is expected
to take about {{MINUTES_PER_PROJECT}} minutes, giving an estimated total burden
of {{ESTIMATED_TOTAL_MINUTES}} minutes if you reviewed them all. You may review
all or only some of the projects.

Contributors may separately choose to be acknowledged by name in the resulting
paper. Acknowledgement is optional and will not depend on reviewing every
project, agreeing with the classifications, or allowing quotations.

At this stage we are asking only whether you might be interested. No survey or
project-review link is included. If you are interested, please reply Yes and we
will send the participant information and separate project links. A No is also
helpful and will end the approach.

Kind regards,

{{STUDY_NAME}} team  
{{STUDY_EMAIL}}
