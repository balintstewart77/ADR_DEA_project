approval, will contain the final registration PDF. It must not contain coder
assignments, contact details, or post-registration amendments.

and collaborator-review candidate. It is prospective with respect to the formal
validation sample and formal coding, but it is not the final frozen or
registered protocol. The preceding v0.10 and v0.9 candidates are preserved as
superseded, unregistered history.

Final freeze remains conditional on completion and debrief of the excluded
pilot, closure of the dated pilot-feedback log, Jo's final substantive review,
propagation of resulting changes, and final package QA. No official active or
reserve sample draw is authorised under this status. The canonical
machine-readable status and exact protocol provenance are in
`../../preregistration_artifact_manifest.csv`.
